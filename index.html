      Your wins live only on this device

      Entries are stored in your browser — not on a server. They stay private, but they don't sync across devices. If you capture wins on your laptop, they won't appear on your phone.

      Recommended: pick one device as your "home base." Use the export button to back up to Markdown or CSV occasionally.

      💡 Installing this app to your home screen (Share → Add to Home Screen on iOS) helps protect your data from being auto-cleared by your browser.

      How do I get one?
      1. Go to console.anthropic.com and sign in (or create an account).
      2. In the left sidebar, click API Keys.
      3. Click Create Key, give it a name like "Wins app," and hit Create.
      4. Copy the key that starts with sk-ant- — you'll only see it once.
      5. Paste it into the field above and click Save.

      Heads up: Anthropic requires a small prepaid balance ($5 minimum) to use the API. Each brain dump costs roughly $0.001 — $5 covers thousands of entries.
